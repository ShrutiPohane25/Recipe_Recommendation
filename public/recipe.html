Paneer Tikka - Step-by-Step

  1. Mix yogurt with spices and lemon juice.
  2. Marinate paneer and veggies for 30 mins.
  3. Skewer paneer and grill or bake until golden.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Ingredients:

Paneer, Capsicum, Tomato, Onion


Nutrients:

Protein: 18g, Calories: 320, Fat: 22g

Chickpea Sandwich - Step-by-Step

  1. Mash boiled chickpeas with yogurt and spices.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and grated cheese.
  3. Spread on bread slices and toast lightly.
  4. Serve with ketchup or chutney.

Ingredients:

Chickpea, Yogurt, Tomato, Cheese, Bread


Nutrients:

Carbohydrates: 40g, Calories: 300, Fiber: 8g, Protein: 14g

Vegetable Khichdi - Step-by-Step

  1. Wash rice and lentils.
  2. Chop and add vegetables.
  3. Pressure cook with salt and spices.
  4. Serve hot with ghee or pickle.

Ingredients:

rice, lentils, vegetables


Nutrients:

Carbs: 45g, Calories: 300, Fiber: 5g, Protein: 10g

Egg Cutlets - Step-by-Step

  1. Boil and mash eggs with potato and spices.
  2. Shape into patties and coat with breadcrumbs.
  3. Shallow fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve with ketchup or dip.

Ingredients:

Eggs, Potato, BreadCrumbs, Spices


Nutrients:

Carbs: 20g, Calories: 300, Protein: 10g

Kokum Sharbat - Step-by-Step

  1. Mix kokum syrup with cold water.
  2. Add lemon juice, sugar, and black salt.
  3. Stir well and chill.
  4. Serve with ice cubes.

Ingredients:

Kokum Syrup, Sugar, Black Salt, Lemon juice


Nutrients:

Oreo Milkshake - Step-by-Step

  1. Blend Oreo cookies with milk and sugar.
  2. Add chocolate syrup if desired.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Serve chilled with a straw.

Ingredients:

Oreo Cookies, Milk, Sugar, Chocolate


Nutrients:

Carbohydrates: 45g, Calories: 350, Fiber: 2g
